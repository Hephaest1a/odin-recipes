Zuppa Toscana

Description

A warming creamy soup based on Tuscan Soup Minestra di pane

This version follows the recipe Olive Garden's take on Tuscan Soup, and is somewhat inauthentic. Nevertheless, this soup is hearty and delicious on a cold winter's days

Ingredients

  • Russet potatoes - 3
  • Kale - 0.5 bunch/chopped
  • Diced Bacon slices- 8
  • Italian sausage - 1lb
  • Chicken stock - 32oz
  • Diced Yellow onion - 1
  • Flour
  • Coconut Milk/cream - 1 cup
  • Minced Garlic - 4 cloves
  • Paprika

Steps

  1. Brown italian sausage on medium high in a large saute pan and remove from pan
  2. Cook diced bacon in sausage grease and remove from pan
  3. Saute diced onions until golden brown and translucent
  4. Add minced garlic and saute until fragrant
  5. Add spices and flour and oil and cook for 30 seconds
  6. Deglaze with chicken stock
  7. Add diced potatoes
  8. Heat mixture to near boil
  9. Turn down heat to low and simmer until potates are fork tender
  10. Stire in meat, cream, and kale
  11. Season to taste
