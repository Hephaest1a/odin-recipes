Chicken Paprikash

Description

A popular Hungarian dish using quartered chicken

The chicken is simmerered in a sauce that starts with a roux, and contains a generous amount of paprika to give the sauce it's signature color

This version is inspired by Captain American Civil War, and is Wanda-approved (hopefully)

Ingredients

  • Boneless skinless chicken thighs - 2 lbs/chopped
  • Vegetable oil - 3 tbsp
  • Chicken stock - 2 cups
  • Yellow onion - 1/chopped
  • Flour - 1/2 cup
  • Coconut Milk/cream - 1 cup
  • Hungarian Paprika - 3 tbsp
  • Cayenne pepper - 1/8 tsp
  • Salt - 1/2 tsp
  • Black pepper - 1/2 tsp

Steps

  1. Combine flour, paprika, salt, and pepper
  2. Dredge chicken in flour and save leftover flour
  3. Heat oil in large saute/skillet pan over medium high heat
  4. Brown chicken on both sides, 3 mins each side and remove once done
  5. Add onion, cayenne and remaining paprika and saute onion for 2 mins
  6. Return chicken to pan and add chicken stock
  7. Bring pan to boil and reduce heat to medium low
  8. Simmer mixture for 45 minsor until chicken is done
  9. In a separate bowl, mix leftover flour, milk, and 1/2 cup of liquid from skillet until smooth
  10. Add mixture to pan and simmer for 5 mins
  11. Adjust seasoning to taste
